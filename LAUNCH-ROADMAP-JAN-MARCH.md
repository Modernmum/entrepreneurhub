## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Not enough beta signups (Jan)

**Mitigation:**
- Start outreach early (Jan 1, not Jan 15)
- Offer founding member pricing ($997 vs $1,200)
- Extend beta to 35-40 users if needed
- Leverage LinkedIn for additional reach

### Risk 2: Poor testimonials/results (Feb)

**Mitigation:**
- Heavy onboarding support
- Weekly check-ins to ensure success
- Help users get quick wins in Week 1
- Offer custom setup if they're struggling

### Risk 3: Low conversion at $1,500 (March)

**Mitigation:**
- Offer payment plans ($500/mo for 3 months)
- Annual discount ($15K = 17% off)
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Case studies proving ROI
- Can lower to $1,200 if market pushes back

### Risk 4: Technical issues at scale

**Mitigation:**
- Stress test with beta users first
- Cap launch at 100 users for quality
- Dedicated support person by March
- Infrastructure scaling plan ready
