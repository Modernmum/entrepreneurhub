# Unbound.team - Final Pricing Strategy
**Based on Competitive Analysis - November 30, 2025**

---

## Executive Summary

After analyzing 20+ competitors across AI content (Jasper, Writer), lead generation (Apollo), market research (Wynter), and web automation (Browse AI), we've identified the optimal pricing structure:

**Entry Point:** $39/month (annual) / $49/month (monthly)
**Model:** Hybrid (base subscription + credits/jobs)
**Free Tier:** Generous enough to compete with Browse AI (50+ credits)
**Annual Discount:** 20% (industry standard)

## Pricing FAQ (for website)

**Q: What are credits?**
A: Credits are our flexible usage currency. Different tasks cost different credits (e.g., lead generation = 10 credits, 1,000 words of content = 2 credits). This lets you use Unbound.team however you need each month.

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits?**
A: You can either upgrade to a higher plan, buy credit top-ups ($20 for 100 credits), or wait for your monthly reset. We'll alert you at 80% usage.

**Q: Can I switch plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades are prorated; downgrades take effect next billing cycle.

**Q: What's the difference between monthly and annual?**
A: Annual plans save 20% and lock in your rate. Monthly plans offer flexibility but cost more. All plans include the same features.

**Q: Do unused credits roll over?**
A: On Starter and Growth, unused credits expire monthly. On Pro, you get unlimited credits (fair use policy - no expiration).

**Q: How is this cheaper than buying separate tools?**
A: Instead of paying for Apollo ($59), Jasper ($49), Wynter ($798), landing page builder ($29), and email tool ($30) separately (= $965/mo), you get all five in Unbound.team Growth for $119/mo. That's 87% savings.

**Q: What's "fair use" on the Pro plan?**
A: Unlimited credits means you can run as many jobs as you need for your business. We only throttle if usage appears to be reselling our service or abuse (e.g., 10,000+ jobs/day).
