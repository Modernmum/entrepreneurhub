# Unbound.team - Competitive Pricing Analysis & Strategy
**Research Date:** November 30, 2025

---

## Executive Summary

After analyzing 15+ competitors across AI lead generation, content creation, and market research automation, we've identified a **significant pricing opportunity** for Unbound.team. Most competitors charge $50-$300/month for single-feature tools, while Unbound.team offers 5+ integrated solutions.

**Key Insight:** Hybrid pricing (subscription + usage) shows 21% higher growth than pure subscription models in 2025.

## Recommended Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
**Goal:** Acquire first 100 paying customers

**Pricing:**
- Free plan: Generous limits (10 jobs/mo)
- Starter: $39/mo (early bird pricing, normally $49)
- Growth: $99/mo (early bird pricing, normally $149)
- Pro: $249/mo (normally $299)

**Why:** Lower prices to overcome "new product" hesitation

---

### Phase 2: Validation (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Achieve product-market fit, gather data

**Actions:**
- Raise prices to normal levels
- Grandfather early adopters at launch pricing
- A/B test credit-based pricing with 20% of new users
- Analyze which features drive conversions

**Metrics to Track:**
- Free to paid conversion rate (target: 8%+)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Churn rate (target: <5%/month)
- Feature usage patterns

---

### Phase 3: Optimization (Months 7-12)
**Goal:** Maximize revenue per customer

**Actions:**
- Introduce annual plans (2 months free = 17% discount)
- Add enterprise tier with custom pricing
- Implement usage-based upsells
- Launch referral program (1 month free per referral)

**Potential Hybrid Model:**
- Base plan + usage credits for overage
- Example: Starter at $49/mo includes 50 jobs, then $1/job after
